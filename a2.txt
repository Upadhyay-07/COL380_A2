Introduction
Histogram equalization is used to improve the contrast of 3D color data by
manipulating their intensity distributions. In this assignment, you will be given
a 3D point cloud.
Each point cloud consists of a set of N points:
P = {p1 , p2 , . . . , pN }
Each point pi has:
• 3D spatial coordinates (xi , yi , zi )
• An intensity value Ii ∈ {0, 1, . . . , 255}
Unlike regular images, point clouds are not arranged on a 2D grid. Instead,
neighborhood relationships are defined by spatial proximity in 3D space.
Your task is to implement local histogram equalization on graphstructured data using CUDA using three different approaches and then compare their efficiency in your reports. You are encouraged to use openmp for host
side optimizations.
